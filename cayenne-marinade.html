Cayenne Chicken Marinade

You will need:

  • 1kg of chicken breast
  • Herbs de provence
  • Cayen pepper
  • Onion salt
  • 1 tbspn olive oil
  • 1 tbspn soy sauce

The instructions are:

  1. Make the marinade, put oil, soy sauce and spices in a container add chicken and shake. Liberally cover the chicken
  2. Let the marinade sit for 30 minutes
  3. Add to airfryer for 24 minutes at 180 celsius.
  4. Turn halfway through

Enjoy :)
