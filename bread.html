Homemade Keto Bread

To begin with you will need the following:

  • Loaf pan
  • Pan liners or baking paper
  • Whisk
  • Almond flour 230g
  • Ground flaxseed (brown) 40g
  • Baking powder, 3 tsp
  • Guar gum 1 tsp
  • Olive spread 125g
  • Eggs 4
  • Water, 125ml

The instructions are as follows:

  1. Preheat oven in 180Ëšc
  2. Mix almond flour, baking powder and guar gum in a seperate bowl
  3. Whisk 4 eggs to bowl
  4. Melt olive spread and add to bowl
  5. Add ground flaxseed
  6. Add 125ml of warm/boiling water to bowl
  7. Whisk again, make sure it's somewhat frothy
  8. Add the almound flour, mixed with baking powder + guar gum to bowl
  9. Mix until it's a batter
  10. Add the batter to a lined bread tin (I use loaf pans and pan liners for convenience)
  11. Add to oven for 40 minutes, remove then let sit for 30 mins.

Enjoy your freshly baked almond bread! It's delicious!!!
