Pasta Bolognese with Turkey mince

To get started you will need these ingredients:

  • 1 can of chopped tomatoes
  • 500g of turkey mince
  • 75g of wholegrain pasta (dry)
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 red onion
  • pots for cooking the pasta and frying the mince

What to do with the ingredients:

  1. Dice the onion
  2. Press the garlic
  3. Heat a pot for the mince for a few minutes, heat the mince until brown
  4. Add the onions, stir for 3 minutes, then add garlic and stir for an additional 2 minutes.
  5. Add can of chopped tomatoes stir, reduce heat to 2 on hob and let cook for 40 minutes.

Enjoy your food!!! :]
